Simply Midwifery

I've been a midwife for too long (37+ years), but I love the work. Well, its not work really because I love making connections and helping people out... No nonsense midwifery based in the Far North of New Zealand. Primary unit birthing and occasional home births. Clinic or home visits. Always do your very best with what you have available!
